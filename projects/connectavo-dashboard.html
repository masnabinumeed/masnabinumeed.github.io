Summary

I worked as the UI/UX Designer for connectavo GmbH, a geman startup working on predicting industrial maintenance. I worked on the dashboard design of the CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System).

My role

  • User research
  • Interface design
  • Prototyping
  • User testing
  • Design system

Acknowledgments

Special thanks to Syed Ali Hasan for teaching me how to work with code.

Project and role

connectavo digitizes the end-to-end maintenance process of industrial machinery and thereby changes how information and communication is handled inside the factory - all packaged into an easy-to-use SaaS tool with little implementation effort.

I worked on the front-end development and user experience improvements of the tool using customer feedback and requirements. This involved making sketches and wireframing, making high fidelity mockups, HTML / CSS work for the front-end and user testing.

Screens of the portal are not shown on client's discretion.

Besides this, I worked on the brand identity of the company and website development.
