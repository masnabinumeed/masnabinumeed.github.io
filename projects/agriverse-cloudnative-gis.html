Combining Remote Sensing with AI/ML for Precision Agriculture and Interventions

Client

Introduction

Agriverse is a cloud-native GIS tool that combines remote sensing data from satellites and UAV drones with analysis from AI/ML frameworks for precision agriculture.

It aims to provide farmers, industry, agri-techs and consumers with a platform for insights and recommendations.

It is a part of the GreenAI initiative by the Pakistan Airforce, PSDP and Ministry of Defence.
