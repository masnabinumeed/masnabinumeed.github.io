Website design

Redesigning the add listing form for property classifieds website

Brand

Summary

I worked as a Product Design consultant to work on rewamping the new property listing form and the remarketing landing page.

My role

  • User research
  • Interface design
  • Prototyping

Problem statement

Zameen.com is an online platform for purchase, sale and rent of real estate properties in Pakistan.

I was approached as a Product Design consultant for the following tasks:

  • Redesigning the Ul and UX of “Add Property" form on mobile and desktop versions of the company's website.
  • Examining existing bounce rates of the property remarketing pages on mobile and desktop versions of the website and proposing designs to reduce it.

Design and process

For the ‘Add Property’ form, I used the existing form as my basis and worked on reducing or combining similar fields. For the amenities section, I looked into the historic data of each input field to decide if it should be included or not.

I made the prototype in Adobe XD. Material Design was used as the framework, since the company was keen on adopting it. I chunked the form into smaller steps to reduce the cognitive load of the process. A working prototype was finalized and shared for review.

For redesign of the remarketing page, I designed the argument that bounce rate was not an effective metric to measure performance and the reason for the high bounce rate was lack of proper event tracking.

I then made a new landing page for retargeting ads whose key metric to measure was Pages/session rather than bounce rate. The user is shown the property they clicked on the ad at the top in full view. Below it, they are shown similar properties which leads them to more pages on the website.
