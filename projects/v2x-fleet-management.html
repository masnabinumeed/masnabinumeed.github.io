Web app design

Unified Fleet and Job Management Portal

Brand

Summary

Utilizing Jobs-To-Be-Done framework to unify Fleet and Job management under one consolidated portal.

My role

  • User flows
  • Wireframing
  • Interface design
  • Prototyping

User flows

V2X Network wanted to offer a unique combination of a Fleet management and Job management on their new platform, Connectia. I applied the Jobs-To-Be-Done (JTBD) framework to make user flow diagrams for the system. This was later adopted into the Information Architecutre of the web app.

Wireframing

In the second phase, the earlier user flows were used to make wireframes. These were used for collaboration with V2X Network and quick iterations to get to the final direction on the navigation, layouts and similar patterns.

Limited examples are shown below for confidentiality.

Final Mockups

V2X Network wanted to use green in the interface to reflect on a more environment friendly brand. They also wanted the designs to appeal to a more mature/aged audience of managerial employees in the automation industry. The final mockups were then prototyped into a clickable prototype for interaction reference and to onboard industry stakeholders.
